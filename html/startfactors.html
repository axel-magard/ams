AMS Startfactor Calculation

Startfactor Calculation in AMS computes the start factors of Workcenter and Operations for a selected Product Type and to be specified Daily Output in terms of

Relative Load SOB
Number of parts started at given Workcenter or Operation for each part started in the manufacturing line
Relative Load Mfg
Number of parts started at given Workcenter or Operation for each part shipped from the manufacturing line
Overall Load Per Hour
Number of parts started at given Workcenter or Operation per hour
Overall Load Per Shift
Number of parts started at given Workcenter or Operation per shift
Overall Load Per Day
Number of parts started at given Workcenter or Operation per day
