AMS Workcenter

An AMS Workcenter represents a physical resource in a manufacturing line, typically a collection of machines or work places where manufacturing operations are performed.

An AMS Workcenter has the following attributes

Capacity
number of work stations (machines or work places)
Batch size
how man parts are processed in parallel at the same time
Coefficient of Variation of the Mean Cycle Time of manufacturing operations performed at this workcenter
Reliability ( or Availability )
percentage of time work center is available for manufacturing
Mean Down Time
mean duration of periods of unavailability
Coefficient of Variation of Mean Down Time

In addition the following cost and resource specific attributes can be defined:

Depreciation
yearly Depreciation, a fixed cost
Cost of Service & Maintenance
yearly cost for Service & Maintenance
Operation Cost
Operational cost per unit of time a part is processed
Floor Space needed for this workcenter
Special Area space needed for this workcenter
A flag indicating whether Floor Space is used as buffer space ( = space for parts waiting )
Cost of Scheduled Maintenance
Cost of Un-scheduled Maintenance

To change Workcenter attributes either right-click on a Workcenter in the tree view of model artefacts on the left side of the screen and select "Edit" ...

... or right-click on "Workcenter" and select "Edit".

The "Edit Workcenter" dialog also allows to add or delete workcenter. This can also be achieved by right-clicking on a workcenter and selecting "Add" or "Delete".

When a Workcenter is deleted it is required to assign all operations assigned to that Workcenter to a different Workcenter. Thus an "Operation Assing ..." dialog will appear for each operation asking to do so.
