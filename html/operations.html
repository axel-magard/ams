AMS Operations

An AMS Operation represents a step in a Process Flow of a Product Type

It has to be assigned to a Workcenter and basically describes an operation performed for building a part or batch of parts.

An AMS Operations has the following attributes:

  • Cycle Time: duration of that operation
  • Split/Join Factor: can be used to define that multiple parts will be created for one incoming part at that operation (Split/Join Factor > 1), or multiple incoming parts will be put together to form one parts leaving that operation (Split/Join Factor < 1).
  • Workcenter: Workcenter this operation is assigned to

In addition the following cost and resource specific attributes can be defined:

  • Material: Material Cost to process this part or batch of parts at this operation
  • Scrapping Cost: Cost occuring when scrapping a part or batch of parts at this operation
  • Handling Time: Time needed to handle a part or batch of parts at this operation before and after it can be processed
  • Inspection Time: Inspection Time needed to check a part or batch of parts at this operation

To change attributes of an Operation either right-click on an Operation in the tree view of model artefacts on the left side of the screen and select "Edit" ...
